# Frequently Asked Questions

## What is BrainstormX Lab?
BrainstormX Lab is a student-led technical team focusing on AI multimedia restoration and lightweight model deployment.

## What are your main research areas?
Our core research includes GAN-based image restoration, old media digital recovery, and edge AI optimization.

## How to contact the lab?
You can reach us via the email displayed on the homepage.

## Can I cooperate or join the lab?
We welcome students and developers interested in AI technologies to communicate and cooperate with us.
